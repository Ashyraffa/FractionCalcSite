FractionCalc


FractionCalc is a free and open-source fraction calculator that was made in hope to help students, especially middle-schoolers to learn math, especially fractions and decimals.



Linux Version?


FractionCalc is now available for Linux! Download the latest version here.



Simple, Fast and Powerful.


  • Convert to decimal to fraction, or vice versa quickly.
  • Calculate up to 4 fractions at once blazingly fast
  • Completely Free, no ads, spyware, or user tracking.
  • Open-Source, so you can fork it and modify it as much as you want.
